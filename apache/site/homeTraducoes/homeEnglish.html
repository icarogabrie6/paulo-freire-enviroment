Welcome to this virtual environment. We tried to organize our site with a welcoming teaching style that is dynamic and appealing to the eye. We hope you have a pleasant search experience and that the content available here is useful to you. This website is structured, basically, into two possible paths for research navigation, either "Work” or "Timeline".

By choosing the “Work” path, you can browse through Paulo Freire's articles, and are able to view them in chronological or alphabetical order. Also in this section, you can watch interviews and view images.

When navigating the “Timeline” path, you’ll have a linear view of Paulo Freire's life and his work. You can see Freire’s photos, handwritten manuscripts, audiotapes, videos and albums, as well as passages about remarkable moments in Freire's life and famous quotations he has authored.

If you want to do a more advanced search, go to the "Archive" link and browse through Paulo Freire's complete repository of books.

Enjoy. Share and promote this site. Come back often!
